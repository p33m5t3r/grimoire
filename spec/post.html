Group (Abstract Algebra)

A set G with a binary operation • that satisfies:

  • Closure: For all a,b ∈ G, a•b ∈ G
  • Associativity: (a•b)•c = a•(b•c)
  • Identity: There exists e ∈ G where e•a = a•e = a
  • Inverse: For each a ∈ G, there exists b ∈ G where a•b = b•a = e
